# Getting Started Guide

Welcome! This guide will help you get up and running quickly.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- An active account
- Access to the dashboard
- Basic understanding of the product

## Quick Start

### Step 1: Create Your Profile

1. Log in to your account
2. Navigate to Settings > Profile
3. Fill in your information
4. Click Save

### Step 2: Configure Your Workspace

Set up your workspace preferences:

- Choose your default theme (light or dark)
- Set notification preferences
- Configure timezone settings

### Step 3: Explore Features

Take a tour of the main features:

- **Dashboard**: Overview of your account activity
- **Projects**: Create and manage your projects
- **Analytics**: View usage statistics and reports
- **Settings**: Customize your experience

## Next Steps

Once you're familiar with the basics:

1. Review the [User Guides](../user-guides/) for detailed instructions

3. Join our community forum for tips and support
