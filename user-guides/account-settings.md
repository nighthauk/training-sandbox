# Account Settings

Manage your account preferences and security settings.

## Profile Settings

### Personal Information

Update your profile details:

- Full name
- Email address
- Profile picture
- Bio/Description
- Location
- Website/Social links

### Notification Preferences

Choose how you want to be notified:

**Email Notifications:**
- Project updates
- Team invitations
- System announcements
- Weekly digest

**In-App Notifications:**
- Real-time updates
- Mentions and comments
- Task assignments

## Security Settings

### Password Management

Change your password regularly for security:

1. Go to Settings > Security
2. Click **Change Password**
3. Enter current password
4. Enter new password (minimum 12 characters)
5. Confirm new password
6. Click **Update Password**

**Password Requirements:**
- Minimum 12 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Enable 2FA for enhanced security:

1. Go to Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication
2. Click **Enable 2FA**
3. Scan QR code with authenticator app
4. Enter verification code
5. Save backup codes in a secure location

**Supported Authenticator Apps:**
- Google Authenticator
- Authy
- Microsoft Authenticator

### Active Sessions

View and manage your active login sessions:

- See all devices where you're logged in
- Review login locations and times
- Revoke access to suspicious sessions

## Privacy Settings

### Data Sharing

Control what information is shared:

- Profile visibility (public/private)
- Activity visibility
- Search engine indexing
- Analytics opt-in/opt-out

### Account Deletion

To permanently delete your account:

1. Go to Settings > Privacy
2. Scroll to **Delete Account**
3. Review what will be deleted
4. Enter your password
5. Confirm deletion

**Note:** Account deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed.

## Billing & Subscription

Manage your subscription and payment methods in Settings > Billing.

For billing questions, contact support@example.com.
